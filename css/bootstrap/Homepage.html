For warm tone

In makeup, warm tones refer to colors with underlying hints of yellow, gold, peach, or orange. When products (like foundation, eyeshadow, or lipstick) are described as warm, it means they lean toward the sun-kissed, earthy, or fiery end of the color spectrum

For cool tone

Cool-toned makeup features products formulated with blue, pink, grey, or purple-ish pigments. It either refers to complexion products (like foundations and concealers) that match skin with cool undertones, or to a popular, frosty makeup color palette (mauves, berries, and icy shades) that contrasts beautifully with any skin tone.

For Nutural

"Natural makeup" generally refers to two different things: a cosmetic style (the "no-makeup makeup look") or a category of beauty products (clean, plant-based cosmetics).
